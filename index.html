About FarmLink

Purpose-Built for Ethiopian Coffee

FarmLink is not adapted from another crop or imported from another continent. It is a deeply integrated digital infrastructure that connects the three critical stages of the coffee supply chain into one seamless, offline-capable system.

🇪🇹

Built on the Ground

Developed in Ethiopian washing stations with producers and cooperatives, handling local nuances that generic platforms overlook.

📡

Offline-First

The entire field workflow—registration, purchasing, receipting—functions perfectly offline. Connectivity is optional, not required.

🔗

Architectural Traceability

Traceability is not a feature added later. It is the natural architecture of the platform, emerging from daily operations automatically.

📊

Deep Data

Granular operational data—yields, losses, farmer productivity—that surface-level tools miss, fueling analytics and buyer confidence.

Key Capabilities

One Platform, Three Stages, Complete Visibility

FarmLink digitizes the entire operational workflow, creating one continuous, verifiable digital record from farm plot to export container.

01

Unified Smallholder Sourcing

Digital farmer identities, real-time purchasing with scale integration, instant receipt printing, and automated payment reconciliation—working at the speed of a queueing farmer, online or offline.

  • Digital Registration
  • GPS Farm Mapping
  • Instant Receipts
  • Payment Integration
02

End-to-End Operational Traceability

Every cherry purchase links to a geolocated farmer. Every processing lot connects to source deliveries. Every export container carries digital history. One unbroken chain of custody, queryable in real time.

  • Batch Tracking
  • Chain of Custody
  • Source Verification
  • Audit Trail
03

Offline-First Intelligence

The mobile app operates at full capacity without internet. Weighing, receipting, and verification all function offline. Data syncs intelligently when connectivity returns—no batch uploads, no manual transfers.

  • Full Offline Mode
  • Auto-Sync
  • No Data Loss
  • Cloud Backup
04

Processing Visibility & Inventory Integrity

Track coffee through pulping, fermentation, drying, hulling, and grading—with the link back to producer and plot maintained. Yield and loss calculated automatically. Inventory reconciled against sourced volumes.

  • Stage Tracking
  • Yield Analytics
  • Quality Grading
  • Inventory Control
05

Automated Trade & Compliance Readiness

Export documentation generated from live operations. EUDR due diligence pulls geolocation data automatically. Certification evidence for Rainforest Alliance, Organic, and Fair Trade organized and exportable on demand.

  • EUDR Ready
  • Certification Support
  • Buyer Reports
  • Export Docs
06

Producer Empowerment by Design

Every farmer receives a permanent digital profile, instant transaction receipts, and verifiable payment history. This strengthens bargaining position, provides credit access evidence, and formalizes their supply chain role.

  • Digital Identity
  • Payment History
  • Receipt Records
  • Credit Readiness
What Makes FarmLink Different

Not Easily Copied. Not Easily Matched.

FarmLink's competitive advantage lies in the depth of its integration, the reality of its offline capability, and its architectural commitment to traceability.

🏗️

Architectural Traceability

Traceability is not a module or a feature. It is the fundamental architecture of the platform. Every data point connects by design—emerging automatically from daily operations.

📡

Genuine Offline-First

Not "offline when possible." The entire field workflow functions without connectivity as the default mode. This is the foundation—without it, remote sourcing cannot truly be digitized.

🇪🇹

Built for Ethiopia

Developed on the ground in Ethiopian washing stations with Ethiopian producers. It handles local sourcing structures, payment flows, and operational realities that adapted software cannot.

🔐

Deep, Not Surface Data

Captures granular operational data—processing yields, losses, farmer-level productivity—that simple data collection tools miss entirely. This depth powers real analytics and genuine buyer confidence.

🔗

One System, Not a Patchwork

Replaces fragmented sourcing apps, processing spreadsheets, and trade documentation tools with a single synchronized platform where data flows seamlessly across all three stages.

Compliance as Output, Not Project

EUDR and certification documentation are generated from live operational data, not assembled retrospectively. Compliance becomes a routine output of doing business.
