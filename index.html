What We Do

Digital Sourcing

Register farmers, record cherry purchases, and print receipts—even offline. Built for washing station gates.

Processing Tracker

Trade Ready

How It Works

From farm to container in three steps

FarmLink connects what happens at the washing station to what your buyers need to see.

1

Capture at Source

Clerks record purchases on the mobile app. Digital scales, auto-calculations, instant receipts.

2

Track Through Processing

Each lot stays linked to its growers. Monitor quality, yields, and inventory from one dashboard.

3

Share With Buyers

Export traceability reports, compliance docs, and shipment data—all pulled from daily operations.
